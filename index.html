Can't watch some of the videos?

  1. Download this Chrome extension or this Firefox extension.
  2. Open the source page of the video that doesn't work (click on the name in the corner of the video player).
  3. Set the extension to China.
  4. Reload this page.

Help us

  1. Download our Chrome extension to suggest videos.
  2. Look for videos in one of the websites on the right. Here are a few keywords to get you started:
    • 动漫 (cartoon)
    • 童話故事 (fairy tale)
    • 食谱 (recipe)
    • 如何 (how to)
  3. Click on the extension button to submit the video you are currently watching.
